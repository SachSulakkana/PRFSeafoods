OUR CAPACITY


The company has state of the art cold storage facilities with total capacity of 1,450.00 Metric Tons. Cold storage facilities contain four refrigerated storages which maintain temperature levels from – 20°C to – 30°C.
The newly built local bait fish processing plant in end of 2020 has a production capacity of 16 metric tons per day with under-40°C temperature level blast freezing facility.
